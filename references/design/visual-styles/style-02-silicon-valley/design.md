# Style 2: Silicon Valley

Load when the user chooses visual style `2`.

## Use For

Fundraising, AI startups, product launches, and investor roadshows.

## Keywords

Growth, innovation, future, capital, speed, disruption.

## Visual Direction

- Huge titles and minimal content.
- One claim per page.
- Large numbers and strong modern rhythm.
- Reference mood: Airbnb, Stripe, OpenAI, Y Combinator, Sequoia.

## TGO/GTLC Handling

- Use light pages for readability and dark/cover pages for bold claims.
- Keep copy extremely short.
- Prefer a sharp narrative arc over dense explanation.

## Content Expression

### Image Treatment

- Use only confirmed user photos, licensed/approved images, or approved AI-generated visuals. Favor one bold product, customer, maker, or future-state image per key claim.
- Crop aggressively, keep a light-and-dark rhythm, and use clean contemporary color; the image must amplify the page's single assertion.

### Diagram Language

- Use simple growth loops, product flywheels, before/after moments, and crisp directional arrows; keep nodes sparse and highly legible.

### Data Expression

- Make one number, growth curve, milestone, or market comparison dominant; suppress secondary gridlines and explanatory clutter.

### Avoid

- Avoid generic startup handshakes, dense dashboards, multi-image mosaics, and decorative AI visuals that dilute the claim.
