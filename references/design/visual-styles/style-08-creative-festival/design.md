# Style 8: Creative Festival

Load when the user chooses visual style `8`.

## Use For

AI comics/drama, innovation conferences, design conferences, community events, creator conferences.

## Keywords

Creative, young, energetic, breakthrough, entertainment, future culture.

## Visual Direction

- High-saturation contrast and bold typography.
- Visual experiments, illustration/comic elements, motion-graphic feel.
- Reference mood: SXSW, Adobe MAX, Netflix creative events.

## TGO/GTLC Handling

- Keep the brand background and identity layer.
- Allow stronger accent colors and expressive layouts on creative sections.
- Use this selectively when the deck also contains formal/business sections.

## Content Expression

### Image Treatment

- Use only confirmed user photos, licensed/approved images, or approved AI-generated visuals. Favor expressive illustration, community photography, comics, and event energy when they make the idea more memorable.
- Use bold crops, high-saturation contrast, collage or comic framing, and playful overlays while preserving readable copy and the TGO/GTLC identity layer.

### Diagram Language

- Use lively timelines, poster-like journeys, modular comic panels, and energetic arrows; retain a clear reading order despite the expressive layout.

### Data Expression

- Use bold pictorial comparisons, simplified charts, and oversized numbers; annotate the insight directly rather than recreating a dense dashboard.

### Avoid

- Avoid visual noise, illegible type over imagery, random stickers, trend-only effects, and playful treatment on pages requiring formal precision.
