# Style 7: Chinese Business

Load when the user chooses visual style `7`.

## Use For

SOEs, central enterprises, government projects, industrial upgrading, and China technology narratives.

## Keywords

Oriental aesthetics, cultural confidence, China technology, industrial upgrading.

## Visual Direction

- Whitespace, modern ink, red/gold accents, oriental geometry.
- Traditional and technological fusion.
- Keep accents disciplined and content-led.

## TGO/GTLC Handling

- Use TGO/GTLC frames as the base.
- Add Chinese-style accents only if they support the content.
- Avoid heavy ceremonial decoration on normal content pages.

## Content Expression

### Image Treatment

- Use only confirmed user photos, licensed/approved images, or approved AI-generated visuals. Favor authentic industrial, urban, cultural, or team imagery when it grounds the China technology or upgrading narrative.
- Apply modern restrained composition with disciplined red/gold accents or ink-like texture; preserve factual clarity over ceremonial atmosphere.

### Diagram Language

- Use orderly landscape-to-industry-to-capability flows, modern grid geometry, and sparing ink-inspired separators; make policy, industry, and execution relationships explicit.

### Data Expression

- Use clear industry comparisons, regional maps, roadmaps, and milestone charts; reserve red/gold for the decisive metric or strategic priority.

### Avoid

- Avoid generic landmarks, flags as decoration, clichéd calligraphy, heavy red backgrounds, and ornamental motifs that obscure business content.
