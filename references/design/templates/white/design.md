# White Template Design

Load this file when the user chooses white template, dense text/tables, formal documentation, or print-friendly output.

## Asset

- PPTX: `assets/design/templates/white/tgo-gtlc-white.pptx`
- Original source: `GTLC-PPT模版-白底.pptx`
- Visual role: white content pages, dark blue cover/back cover, top-right logo, bottom blue footer strip.

## Layouts

- `封面`: opening cover.
- `1_大标题`: centered section divider.
- `大标题`: section page with body.
- `自我介绍`: speaker/self introduction.
- `目录`: agenda/table of contents.
- `内容1-大字标题`: content page with large title.
- `内容2-小字标题`: default content page.
- `空内容`: blank branded page.
- `封底`: closing/thanks.

## Key Positions

- Cover title: `x=1.800 y=2.544 w=18.684 h=3.103`
- Cover subtitle/body: `x=1.801 y=6.054 w=20.792 h=6.403`
- Standard content title: about `x=105 pt y=179 pt`
- Standard content body: about `x=107 pt y=302 pt`
- Footer strip: `x=0 y=13.388 w=26.667 h=1.612`
- Top-right logo: `x=22.153 y=0.728 w=3.465 h=2.852`

## Use

- Best for dense content, tables, handouts, formal reports, and printable decks.
- Prefer dark text on white background.
- Keep the footer strip and top-right logo clear of body content.
