## Visual Role

LOOP Orange White is a GTLC branch style, not a primary GTLC template. Keep `light`, `white`, and `dark` as the primary GTLC choices. Use this branch when the deck needs a stronger summit, ecosystem, sponsor, or event-promotion feeling.

## Use For

- AI ecosystem and innovation summits.
- Sponsor benefits, partnership proposals, and event promotion.
- City chapter event decks where energy and social spread matter.
- Agenda, speaker intro, data profile, timeline, sponsor tier, and thanks pages.

## Avoid For

- Board reports and formal executive readouts.
- Dense tables, budget sheets, or print-first reports.
- Materials where GTLC must remain strictly in the blue primary brand frame.

## Visual Direction

- Top orange brand bar flush to the slide top.
- Logo placed inside the top bar on the far right.
- White or warm-white canvas with generous whitespace.
- Orange section labels, orange metric highlights, and light rounded panels.
- Subtle ring or motion accents only on cover, section, and summary pages.
- No centered logo on normal pages.
- No small standalone `会` seal behind the logo.

## Customer Choice Guidance

When presenting template options to a customer, show LOOP Orange White as option `D` after the primary GTLC choices:

- `A｜GTLC Light`: default business template
- `B｜GTLC White`: dense report and print-friendly template
- `C｜GTLC Dark`: keynote opening, section, and closing template
- `D｜LOOP Summit`: orange-white summit branch style
